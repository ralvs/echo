Retrieval upgrade next up — with data

The original “make Echo better at saving & retrieving” goal. The baseline says search mostly works; design against the misses the eval surfaces, starting with paraphrase-style queries.

The retrieval hypothesis

It’s probably not the search — it’s the aliases and the missing context.

At ~50 thoughts, semantic search rarely misses outright. The bet: quality degrades from capture-side rot (the same person or project saved under different names fragments the graph) and flat results (a thought comes back without its neighbors — the decision that superseded it, the entity’s other mentions). If the eval confirms this, the fix is alias-safe entity resolution at capture time plus graph context attached to search results — not a fancier retriever.

evidence: alias fragmentation already forced migration 00020 (“fix_people_aliases”) · a thought literally reads “Echo (originally called Engram)”
